### Basic question words

**what** ---- ntrớu 

**who** ---- nau / noau 

**which** ---- aléq

**how** ---- nṓq

**why** ---- nṓq

**where** ---- léq / nléq

**when** ---- Bo léq / 

**how many** ---- maléq / mah léq / seiq (cannot occur with other quantifier)

**how much** ---- maléq / mah léq
